## SENTIMENT SCORE: THE PRACTICAL TOOL

**What it is**:
A continuous feedback loop tracking how your actions influence outcomes.

**Formula**:
```
S(t+1) = S(t) + α·[O(a_t) - B]
```
- S(t) = sentiment score at time t
- α = learning rate
- O(a_t) = outcome of action a at time t
- B = baseline expectation

**Why it matters**:
1. **Compounding Effects**: Small positive actions accumulate
2. **Momentum**: Positive sentiment creates positive opportunities
3. **Feedback**: Reality responds to your patterns
4. **Alignment**: High sentiment = aligned with universal consistency

**How to use**:
1. Track major decisions and outcomes
2. Notice patterns (which actions → positive outcomes?)
3. Double down on what works
4. Abandon what doesn't
5. Trust the compounding

**Connection to h equation**:
```
h = κ · ε · g(e_φ) · (1 - ζ/ζ*) · u · p

High sentiment → increases ε (eligibility)
High sentiment → decreases ζ (brittleness)
High sentiment → increases u (urge/confidence)

Therefore: High sentiment → Higher h → More likely commitments → More opportunities
```
